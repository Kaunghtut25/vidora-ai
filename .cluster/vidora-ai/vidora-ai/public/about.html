About Vidora AI

Built in Yangon, Myanmar — for creators everywhere.

Our Story

Vidora AI was born from a simple frustration: creating professional videos takes too long and costs too much. In 2026, with AI advancing rapidly, we saw an opportunity to give every creator — regardless of budget or technical skill — access to studio-quality video production.

We're especially proud to be the first platform offering natural-sounding Burmese AI voices. Myanmar has a vibrant creator community, and we believe language should never be a barrier to creating world-class content.
